Coffee

(delicious)

Description

Once done you will be enjoying a nice little cup of coffee

Ingredients

  • Coffee grounds of your choice or k-cup
  • Mug of your choice
  • Keurig K-cup coffee maker
  • Reusable K-cup(optional)
  • Creamer of your choice

Steps

  1. Select your coffee of choice and put it into your machine
  2. Fill the mug with creamer first to your likeness(this will mix it for you)
  3. Start the 8oz selection on the machine and fill your mug
  4. Clean or throw out your K-pod depending on which you're using
  5. Enjoy!
