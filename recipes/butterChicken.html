Butter Chicken

(spicy)

Description

This is a Indian dish that goes quite well with rice or na'an. Even a tortilla if you're feeling lazy! It is a perfect blend of spice and seasoning to give you that little kick of heat and an explosion of flavor that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1-1.5lb chicken breast or thigh
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 3-6 serrano peppers(your choice of heat)
  • 1 bell pepper
  • bushel of cilantro
  • 1/3-1/2 size of your palm of ginger root
  • 1 garlic(full clove)
  • 1 can of Tomatos
  • 1 small can of tomato paste
  • heavy cream
  • sour cream
  • turmeric
  • paprika
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • garam masala
  • curry powder
  • chili powder(optional)
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Then dice your onion and throw it in a pot and drizzle in olive oil, cook for a few minutes
  2. You cut up your ginger, garlic, and serrano peppers to fine pieces, throw in all these veggies into the onion pot
  3. Cut up the bell pepper into strips then halfs, save for later
  4. Get as much cilantro as you want ready, save for later
  5. Cut the chicken into bite size pieces, then thow into the onions once onions are nearing brown
  6. Mix up all the spices and throw onto chicken and stir nicely
  7. Pour in as many tomatos as you want(little over half usually)
  8. Pour in a little over half of tomato paste and stir
  9. Pour in heavy cream until just barely covering everything but everything is still visible at the top
  10. Pour in 2 spoon fulls of sour cream
  11. Cook 20 min on 4 heat (low simmer)
  12. Throw in bell pepper and cook for another 10-15 minutes
  13. Turn off heat and throw in cilantro and let stand for 5 minutes
  14. Serve with Rice, Na'an, or tortillas
  15. Dig in!
